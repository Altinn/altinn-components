# Article pages

Build articles using `PageBase`, `Article`, `ArticleHeader`, `ArticleHeader`, `Byline`, `Metadata`, and general purpuse components like `Section`, `Divider`, `Heading` and `Typography` to divide and create hiearchy.

```tsx
<PageBase>
  <Article>
    <Breadcrumbs />
    <ArticleHeader title="Title">
      <Byline />
    </ArticleHeader>
    <Typography />
    <Heading siz="xl" />
    <Section>
      <Heading siz="lg" />
      <Typography />
    </Section>
    <Section>
      <Heading siz="lg" />
      <Typography />
    </Section>
  </Article>
</PageBase>
```
